Manyam Grains Private Limited

 

 

"We supply unpolished premium quality processed millets"

We work in partnership with tribal Farmer Producer Organisations and organisations supporting them.

As a B2B company, Manyam Grains supports small business enterprises, retailers, ready to eat enterprises (bakeries, snack foods) and others by supplying premium quality millet rice and related products consistently. We also, extend technical support on millets to such prospective entrepreneurs.

Millet revival is only possible when the issues related to CONSUMPTION, PRODUCTION, PROCESSING and MARKETS are simultaneously addressed.


MANYAM GRAINS PRIVATED LIMITED

Manyam Grains is a Social Enterprise extending technical support, procuring, processing and marketing naturally grown millets primarily from Tribal Farmers' Organisations in the Forest areas of northern Andhra Pradesh, popularly called "MANYAM".

MILLETS






Millets belong to the grass family and are usually grown in rainfed conditions. They are rich in a huge spectrum of micro nutrients like calcium, iron, phosphorous, magnesium etc.
